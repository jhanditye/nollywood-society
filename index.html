Let's learn about Nigeria

Hello, welcome to the best society at Cambridge University.

We explore Nigerian culture through film.

The Nigerian film industry, commonly referred to as Nollywood , is an ever-growing world with a very different character to Hollywood.

Not only is it deeply engaging and entertaining , but it allows us to appreaciate Nigerian culture, a dominanat force in the experience of the African diaspora here in the UK.

We meet every Friday in the Yusuf Hamied Theatre.
